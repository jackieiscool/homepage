About Me

My name is Jackie Herrlin and I am a full-stack web developer. I specialize in Ruby on Rails, and agile web development.


A friend once stated that "the components that make up Jackie are git, rspec, and bowling." This seemed fairly accurate, though there are quite a few other things I care about.


Education is one of my primary passions in life, which is why I have spent the past 6 months working as an instructor in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive course, teaching others the skills they need to become full-stack web developers.


In addition to teaching others, I also care about education from a personal perspective, and am always looking for news ways to expand my own skills. Most recently I have been trying to delve deeper into javascript, and play with frameworks such as angular and node.
